ABOUT ME

A LITTLE ABOUT ME

I am a drummer currently based out of Austin, TX. Most of my work has been in the Texas Country and Worship genres, but I have a passion for any music that is made with true artisty. I love playing dive bar gigs where the focus is truly just on the music as well as high production shows where I can use things like Ableton Live to heighten the spectacle without losing the artisanal mindset that I like to keep when it comes to music.
